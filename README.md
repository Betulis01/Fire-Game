A 2D top-down survival game. Make it through the night.

## The Hook

Your fire is both your lifeline and your liability. A bigger fire keeps the cold
off you — but it's also a brighter beacon in the dark, drawing more enemies to
your camp. Stay warm and stay hidden are at odds. That tension is the game.

## Core Loop

Survive from dusk to dawn:

- **Warmth** — cold drains your health; stay near heat to recover.
- **Fuel** — the fire burns down; chop trees for wood and feed it to keep it alive.
- **Light** — a torch is your portable, burning leash into the dark.
- **Craft** — gather wood and stone, build tools and weapons (e.g. an axe).
- **Fight** — enemies come from the dark edges at night; swing, knock them back, survive.
- **Dawn** — make it to morning and you win the night.

## Built With

- UnityD
- Capability-component architecture shared across players, enemies, and world objects.
